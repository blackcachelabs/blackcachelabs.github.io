Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 April 2026

Blackcache Labs Ltd respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information when you visit our website, contact us, or use any app, software product, or digital service operated by us where this policy is linked.

1. Information we may collect

  • Your name, email address, and any information you submit through our contact forms or support enquiries.
  • Business or project information you choose to send to us.
  • Basic technical information such as browser type, device type, and usage information where applicable for security, analytics, or service improvement.
  • Information required to provide support, respond to requests, or deliver our services.

2. How we use information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide support.
  • To communicate about products, services, or projects you ask us about.
  • To operate, improve, and secure our website, apps, and software services.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our legitimate business interests.

3. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our website, communications, hosting, analytics, or support systems, where reasonably necessary and subject to appropriate safeguards.

4. Data retention

We keep personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose it was collected, including support, legal, operational, and security needs.

5. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. You may also have the right to object to certain processing or request restriction of processing.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Third-party services

Our website, apps, or services may use third-party providers for hosting, analytics, communications, forms, infrastructure, or other operational functions. Those providers may process personal information on our behalf as needed to deliver their services.

8. Children’s privacy

Our services are not intentionally directed to children unless explicitly stated for a specific product. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in a manner not permitted by law.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
